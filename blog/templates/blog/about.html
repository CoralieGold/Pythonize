About

This website is a project realized during my third and last year at the engineering school IMAC (Paris, France). The goal was to become a specialist in something and to show it other skills. I wanted to be a specialist in Python for the 3D software Maya Autodesk and I decided to show it with a website that contains tutorials.
I tried to make more than a school project: I wanted to create a website that can be usefull for every 3D artist who wants to pythonize his Maya!
I hope you like this website and if you want to know more about me and my projects, you can go to my portfolio.
